What is Internet Candy?

Internet Candy is the collective brand of the Swedish producer and visual artist QUFO teamed up with the rapper Vibe God from Houston, Texas. In November 2019, Vibe God from released his debut album titled "Internet Candy" all produced by QUFO. Since then, Internet Candy has become a record label and home for all of our creations.
